Potato gnocchi

INGREDIENTS

  • Potato (Whole)
  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Dried oregano
  • Olive oil
  • ... 4 nos.
  • ... 2 cups
  • ... to taste
  • ... 1 tsp
  • ... 1 tbsp

PREPARATION

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degree.
  2. Scrub the potatoes and poke all over with a fork.
  3. Place the potatoes on the baking sheet and bake for 1 hour.
  4. Once cool peel the skin off the potato.
  5. Mash the potatoes in a mixing bowl until there are no lumps left.
  6. In another bowl, mix the flour and seasoning.
  7. Make a well in the center.
  8. Add the potatoes gradually to the flour mixture.
  9. Bring the dough together and knead to form smooth dough.
  10. Add more flour if required.
  11. Dust the work surface with more flour.
  12. Make small sections of the dough and roll out into 1/2 inch thick rope.
  13. Cut the rope into 3/4 inch long pieces.
  14. Take each piece and pinch the ends to form an oval ball.
  15. Roll with the tines of the fork to get a striped on one side.
  16. Bring a vessel of hot salted water to a boil.
  17. Drop the gnocchi in batches in the salted water.
  18. Scoop out once the pasta rises to the surface.
  19. Keep aside and add a few drops of oil to prevent them from sticking.
