The Jaipur Kitchen Studio launches with its batch for kids' classes this summer! Come bake with us.

3 day baking course for the kids. All the recipes taught would be eggless.

RECIPES TAUGHT

  • Vanilla Cupcake
  • Chocolate Cupcake
  • Mango Cupcake
  • Rainbow Cake
  • How to prepare icings & frostings
  • Learn the art of Fondant decoration to make your cakes look picture perfect.

CLASS SCHEDULE

Learn your basic skills in bakery this summer with us.

  • From sifting flour to Oven knowledge.
  • We will teach you basic cupcake recipes with different flavours.
  • You will decorate your cupcakes with delicious frostings and fondant toppings.
  • Learn how to make butter cream icing.
  • How to prepare piping bags with different nozzles for decoration.
  • How to make a rainbow cake.
  • Learn the art of Fondant decoration.
  • How to make a dome.
  • Making flowers, roses, butterflies using stencils cutters & fondant tools.
  • How to color the fondant and give it a shine.

All the ingredients and accessories would be provided by us onsite.
